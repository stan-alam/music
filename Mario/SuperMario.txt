So there you have the advanced finger picking style guitar tabs for super mario bros theme song. I think these melodies sound great at slow speeds. The actual game plays the melody very fast. I suggest trying to play it slow and accurate. don't worry about speed at all. Playing along to a metronome is a good idea but the rhythm in the super mario bros game is some sort of latin rhythm which makes playing to a standard beat a little difficult. It's difficult but far from impossible. Other than that just take your time and practice religiously.
